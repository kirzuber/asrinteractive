Overview

Enterprise Assurance Solutions (EAS) as an umbrella covers all Assurance services outside of Core Assurance. Specifically EAS includes Risk Assurance, Capital Markets and Accounting Advisory Services (CMAAS) including IFRS, Actuarial, and Financial Risk Management (FRM). Each of these practice areas has significant market demand and growth opportunities, several of which require close collaboration amongst the specialists across these areas. EAS also supports Core Assurance to transform our audits and other engagements. We will work together as a broader EAS practice to drive clear, distinct value propositions to help our clients and create great experiences for our teams.


Objectives

  • Enhance connectivity and engagement with Elite and Priority accounts and increase EAS penetration on managed account services
  • Enhance EAS connectivity to the entire ASR practice; increase knowledge of EAS offerings across their teams and local offices
  • Optimize sales enablement and culture to enable teams to deliver ‘go-to market’ priority projects; focus on profitable growth via disciplined pricing, opportunity pipeline management, and establishment of Managed Account coverage model
